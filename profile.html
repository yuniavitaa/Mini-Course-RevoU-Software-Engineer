About

Revou is an online training center that offers a variety of bootcamp classes that focus on the needs of the digital technology industry. At RevoU you will study intensively for 14 weeks with various materials and activities that will add to your skills and abilities in the field of digital technology. Bootcamp at RevoU is perfect for those of you who have high motivation to start and improve your career in the digital technology industry. This is because you will learn directly from experts who come from related industry backgrounds.

Vision

RevoU wants to be a place for accelerating careers for anyone with the condition that they have a strong will to learn, regardless of background, level of education, or previous career.

Mission

  1. RevoU provide special training for students to prepare them for the world of work
  2. RevoU provides a full stack class
  3. RevoU provides the opportunity to carry out an apprenticeship for three months in Indonesian, Singaporean or United States companies that work remotely with RevoU.
  4. The program is structured for intensive learning for 3-6 months which is scheduled outside working hours.
  5. Commitment to provide 15-20 hours of classes per week
